COVID-19 Death Forecasts: Visualizing Uncertainty Over Time

This visualization explores how forecasts of cumulative COVID-19 deaths in the United States changed over time. The solid line represents the median prediction, while the shaded region shows uncertainty (2.5th–97.5th percentile range). Use the dropdown or play button to see how predictions evolved across forecast dates.
